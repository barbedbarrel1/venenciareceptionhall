Since 2011

A family venue for Mesa's biggest days

Venecia Reception Hall has been family-owned and operated since 2011. For over 14 years, families from across the Valley have trusted us with their weddings, quinceañeras, sweet 16s, baptisms and graduations. Three party areas, one promise: an elegant celebration that doesn't break the budget.

The spaces

Three halls, one address

Grand hall photo
Hall one · up to 300+ guests

The Grand Hall

Our largest space — a full ballroom with stage area, dance floor and room for over 300 guests. Made for grand quinceañeras and wedding receptions that bring the whole family together.

Middle hall photo
Hall two · mid-size celebrations

The Celebration Room

The sweet spot for sweet 16s, graduations and receptions of 100–200 guests. All the elegance of the grand hall, sized for your guest list.

The Salon

Perfect for baptisms, anniversaries and private parties — an intimate space that still feels like an occasion.
