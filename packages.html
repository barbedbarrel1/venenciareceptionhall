Questions

Frequently asked

How much is the deposit to reserve our date?

A deposit reserves your date and locks in your package price. Call us at 480-206-8626 for current deposit amounts — we're happy to walk you through it.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes — payment plans are available on every package so you can pay toward your balance over time instead of all at once.

How many guests can each hall hold?

We have three party areas ranging from intimate gatherings up to 300+ guests. See The Venue for details on each space.

What's included in each package?

Every package includes hall rental, tables & chairs and setup/cleanup at minimum. Higher tiers add linens, centerpieces, DJ service, catering and more — see the package cards above for what's included in each.

Can we customize a package?

Absolutely. Tell us about your celebration when you reach out and we'll tailor a package to your guest count and budget.
